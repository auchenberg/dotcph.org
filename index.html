The dotCPH initiative

The dotCPH initiative to buy and secure the .CPH gTLD to represent the danish city Copenhagen.

Many major european cities has gotten their own gTLD, like .berlin, .paris and .london but Copenhagen is missing out.

We should change this.


Show your interest


How to make to happen?

The first step is to collect interest. So if you are interested in making the .CPH gTLD reality, please subscribe to the newsletter, and help us share this message on Facebook and Twitter.

If you are a company or organization who are interested in moving this initiative forward, please get in touch with Kenneth Auchenberg.
